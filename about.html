About Us

GroceryHeaven

In the heart of our journey lies GroceryHeaven, a humble venture born from a shared passion for quality and community. Founded with a vision to redefine the grocery shopping experience, we embarked on a mission to provide not just sustenance, but a sanctuary for all who seek the finest provisions and personalized service. From our humble beginnings, we've remained steadfast in our commitment to sourcing the freshest produce, pantry staples, and gourmet delights, all while fostering a sense of belonging within our cherished community. Guided by our values of integrity, excellence, and sustainability, GroceryHeaven continues to flourish as a trusted haven where every visit is an enriching experience, and every customer is embraced as family.

Our Branches

Branch 1: Kandy Branch

GreenHeaven Kandy is situated in the picturesque city of Kandy, surrounded by lush greenery and rich cultural heritage. This branch provides an extensive selection of fresh produce, gourmet items, and daily essentials to meet the needs of both locals and tourists. With its spacious layout and modern amenities, GreenHeaven Kandy ensures a premium shopping experience, blending convenience with the charm of the hill country. Whether you're stocking up on groceries or looking for specialty items, our Kandy store offers a delightful shopping experience in a serene setting

Branch 2: Nugegoda Branch

GreenHeaven Nugegoda is nestled in the bustling suburb of Nugegoda, catering to the vibrant local community. This branch offers a wide variety of fresh produce, gourmet items, and everyday necessities, ensuring that all your shopping needs are met in one convenient location. With its modern design and spacious aisles, GreenHeaven Nugegoda provides a comfortable and efficient shopping experience. Whether you're a busy professional or a homemaker, our Nugegoda store is the perfect spot for all your grocery needs.

Branch 3: Colombo Branch

GreenHeaven Colombo is strategically located in the heart of Colombo, serving the dynamic urban population of the city. This flagship store features an extensive selection of fresh produce, gourmet products, and essential household items. The Colombo branch is designed with a contemporary layout and equipped with the latest amenities to enhance your shopping experience. Whether you're looking for exotic ingredients or daily staples, GreenHeaven Colombo is your go-to destination for quality and convenience in the city.
